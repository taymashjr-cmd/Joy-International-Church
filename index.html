Our Ministries

Church Ministries

Discipleship & Spiritual Growth

These ministries are dedicated to deepening faith and understanding of God's word.

  • Joy Academy: Biblical Foundations, Baptism classes, Leadership development, Church membership, School of Marriage.
  • Clusters Department: Bible Study Programs, Family Week events for stronger family units.
  • Intercession Department: Monthly crossover prayers, prayer request management, all-night prayer meetings.

Connect & Fellowship

Find your tribe and build meaningful relationships with those who share your life stage.

  • Men’s Ministry: Biblical teaching, events to strengthen men in leadership and faith.
  • Women’s Ministry: Supportive community for women to grow in faith and encourage one another.
  • Couples Ministry: Events and programs to strengthen marriages and Christ-centered relationships.

Creative Arts & Worship

Using creative gifts to facilitate worship and seamless service production.

  • Creative Arts Department: Oversight of worship, production, and creative expressions.
  • Worship Team: Leading the congregation in musical praise and worship.
  • Multimedia Team: Audio, visual, and lighting support for smooth service flow.

Outreach & Compassion

Extending love and hope beyond our walls in times of need and celebration.

Compassion Department

Bereavement and sickness support, celebrating births and milestones with church family.

Outreach Department

Evangelism initiatives, community events (e.g., outreach braais), welcoming new visitors.

Our outreach programs are designed to uplift the community, provide care, and make a lasting impact.

Join us in spreading hope and compassion through active involvement in our initiatives.

Operations & Support

Teams that work behind the scenes to support the church’s vision and infrastructure.

Special Projects Department

Managing key initiatives, internal projects, and coordinating fundraising to support the vision.

Operations

Facilities, logistics, scheduling, and event coordination to ensure smooth ministry delivery.

Support Teams

Administration, volunteers, tech, and multimedia teams supporting worship and programs.

About us

Welcome to TheChurch

At Joy,We proclaim Jesus Christ as the head of the Church and the living Word of God. Our mission is to establish His Kingdom by teaching the Word with practical application, expressing love through giving, and restoring families to transform lives and communities. We are rooted in spiritual values of faith, hope, and love—grounded in the Word, expecting God's will, and reaching out to others with compassion. Our life values center on empowering people to use their God-given gifts, strengthening families, practicing faithful stewardship, and living with integrity. Everything we do reflects our desire to live out God's truth and love in every area of life.
